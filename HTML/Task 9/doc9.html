SEO Explanation

Defines the page's title, crucial for SEO as it appears in search engine results and browser tabs.

meta name="description": Provides a brief summary of the page content, helping search engines understand the page's topic and improving click-through rates.

meta charset="UTF-8": Ensures proper character encoding, preventing text from displaying incorrectly.

meta name="viewport" : Makes the page responsive by adapting its size and layout to various screen widths, crucial for mobile SEO.
