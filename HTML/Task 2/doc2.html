Chicken Biriyani Recipe

Introduction

This Chicken Biryani recipe is a flavorful, aromatic dish made with marinated chicken, fragrant rice, and a blend of spices â€” perfect for any special occasion or a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • onion, tomatoes
  • Coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, biryani masala, salt, and lemon juice. Add chicken pieces, mix well, and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Prepare the rice: Boil water in a pot. Add bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, and salt. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a pan, fry onions until golden. Remove half for garnish. Add tomatoes to the pan and cook until soft. Add marinated chicken and cook till 80% done. Add coriander and mint leaves.
  4. Layer the biryani: In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer chicken at the bottom. Add a layer of rice. Sprinkle fried onions, saffron milk, and ghee. Repeat the layers.
  5. Dum cooking: Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Fluff the biryani with a fork and serve hot with raita and salad.
